## 🎯 Goal Setting Guidelines

### SMART Criteria

Each goal should be:
- **S**pecific - Clear and unambiguous
- **M**easurable - Quantifiable progress
- **A**chievable - Realistic given resources
- **R**elevant - Aligned with mission
- **T**ime-bound - Has a deadline

### Review Process

1. **Weekly**: Update progress during Sunday briefing
2. **Monthly**: Review and adjust quarterly goals
3. **Quarterly**: Set new quarterly goals
4. **Annually**: Review and set annual goals
